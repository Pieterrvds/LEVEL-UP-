What about my logo?:

From the time I was old enough to start focusing on fitness, my father installed a pull-up bar in the middle of my room. He proudly told me that in his prime, he could do 20 pull-ups in one go. I admired his achievement and became determined to surpass it. That's when my journey began. I made it a daily ritual: 10 pull-ups in the morning, 10 pull-ups before bed, and 10 pull-ups in between my study sessions. Even on days when I didn't feel like doing them, I pushed myself to complete 10 pull-ups. Over the years, my persistence paid off, and I became highly skilled at pull-ups. This experience taught me a powerful lesson: consistency and commitment are the keys to achieving anything. Even more importantly, I learned to enjoy the process. Inspired by my progress, I started teaching others the basics of pull-ups. My passion grew, and I decided to pursue a personal coaching certification. Taking it a step further, I began coaching a group of people using my unique training approach. To make things official, I needed a logo and name for my professional venture. The choice was simple—I wanted something short, clear, and easy to remember. Thats how i came up with the name LEVEL-UP crossfit featuring a pull-up symbol to represent the core of my journey and philosophy!
